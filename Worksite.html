Workshop Site

Elite City Resort, located at the heart of Messinian Bay, at the foot of Taygetos mountain, and just a few minutes away from the beach. It includes a large pool and a restaurant with a terrace on deck. All rooms are decorated in modern, minimalist style and offer satellite TV and hairdryer. They also have laptop-sized safe and minibar. Guests can relax by the large swimming pool, enjoying a drink. The Ef Zin Restaurant serves Greek and international dishes prepared with fresh, local ingredients. The commercial center of Kalamata is only 4 km away.

The Elite Blue Conference Centre is part of the complex Elite City Resort in Kalamata and on the spacious and luxurious conference center of Messinia. The Elite Blue Conference Centre is located in a separate room, just opposite our main house, Elite City Hotel. It consists of eight (8) meeting rooms, all of them interconnected, with their capacity ranging from twenty (20) to one thousand five hundred (1,500) people, in a total area of five thousand (5,000) square meters.
