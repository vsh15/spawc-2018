Places To Visit

Kalamata beach
This is a great Beach if you are inside Kalamata. It is near the city and you can reach it by walking just for a few minutes . It is very clean and welll organized. Many umbrellas are installed there from the local coffee shops. You can just buy a coffee and use one umbrella for free .

Eco Tours
Kalamata is the gateway to Mani, which is only a short drive away. The internationally famous caves at Pyrgos Dyrou are located south of Areopolis and attract tourists from all over the world.

Museum of Messenia
This museum in the center of Kalamata is well worth a visit. Great lighting and artistic displays place the ancient artwork and other relics on historical perspective. Facilities are good for childrens' educational programs.

Church of Agioi
This church is located inside the old city of kalamata. You can visit this place and take the feeling of how Kalamata was hundred of years before. There you can also find many shops which are like an "old style " market.

Ypapanti Church
This church is considered the protector of Kalamata. Each year the day which this church celebrates is also a not working day for all people in Kalamata who also celebrate also for their protector. It is a big old church in great condition. It offers a very big parking just outside of the church for tourists that come with a car

Museum of Military
This museum holds a most interesting collection of military memorabilia from uniforms to weapons, from medals to medical supplies. All is set within the context of the history it is pertinent to. We had a guided tour from a young man from the army who spoke very good English who explained to us all the history surrounding the collection. A hidden treasure that is well worth a visit.
